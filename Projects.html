    BRICS Summit 2023

    As part of the technical production team for the BRICS Summit 2023, I was responsible for vision mixing and live video production during the event’s high-profile sessions.

    My key tasks included:

    Vision mixing: I operated the vision mixer to seamlessly switch between multiple live camera feeds, ensuring smooth transitions and maintaining visual coherence throughout the live broadcast. Graphics integration: I managed the timely display of on-screen graphics, lower-thirds, speaker titles, and visual overlays to enhance the viewer experience and provide context during speeches and discussions. Live broadcast quality control: I closely monitored the visual output in real time, quickly addressing any technical issues and coordinating with camera operators and audio teams to deliver a polished, professional broadcast without interruptions. Through this role, I helped ensure that the summit’s key moments—including speeches from heads of state and panel discussions—were presented clearly and professionally to both local and international audiences.
